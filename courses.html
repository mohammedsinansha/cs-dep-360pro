Department of Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science was established to meet the demand for well-qualified computer professionals and was conceived in the year 1995. The Department started with B.Sc program in 1995, later in 2000 M.Sc. - Computer Science and in 2013 BCA. The Department offers a 3-year degree programme in Computer Science(B.Sc),Computer Applications (BCA) and a 2-year post-graduate degree programme in Computer Science (M.Sc. - Computer Science),
